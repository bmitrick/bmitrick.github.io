Educate

We believe that vaccine education is of the utmost importance, especially in today's environment of medical skepticism. On our Educate page, users can input any vaccine they are interested in receiving to learn more about it. We will link our users to reputable sources such as Vaccines.gov which will allow them to read about the vaccine, its importance, who needs the vaccine, as well as the possible side effects of the vaccine.
