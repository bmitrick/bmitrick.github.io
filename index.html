Welcome!

Vaccination Station is a mobile app focused on vaccine location, consumer edication, and charitable donation. Through our intuitive, Google Maps based Locate page, users can search for vaccine providers based on criteria such as search radius, price range, and insurance provider. Our Educate page allows our users to locate resources from reputable sources such as the Mayo Clinic on specific vaccines and their potential side effects. Our Donate page will provide our users with a direct link to donate to vaccination-centric organizations such as Vaccinate Your Family.
